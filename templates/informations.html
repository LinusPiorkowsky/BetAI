How do the ML models work?
Machine learning models analyze historical match data, extracting key features to identify patterns and trends. By using a Random Forest algorithm, they combine multiple decision trees to create robust and balanced predictions. This approach minimizes overfitting, adapts to new data, and enhances accuracy, making forecasts more reliable for football matches.
What makes a prediction high confidence?
A prediction is considered high confidence when the model identifies strong historical patterns and consistent data trends. Factors like a dominant home team, clear statistical advantages, or strong betting odds alignment contribute to higher confidence levels. Conversely, unpredictable matches with evenly matched teams or volatile performances result in lower confidence scores.
Why only the top 5 leagues?
Predicting lower leagues or mixed competitions like the Champions League is significantly more challenging due to inconsistent data quality, varying team performances, and unpredictable match dynamics. The top 5 leagues offer more stable patterns, reliable statistics, and better historical data, leading to more accurate and consistent predictions.
How often are predictions updated?
Weekend match predictions are released every Friday at 6 PM, while midweek game predictions go live on Tuesday at 2 PM. This ensures the latest data is incorporated, providing the most accurate forecasts before each matchday.
What is your accuracy rate?
The machine learning model achieves 70% overall accuracy in predicting match outcomes. It performs exceptionally well in specific markets, with a 87% recall for Home Wins and 75% recall for X2 (Draw or Away Win). Continuous evaluation using precision, recall, and F1-score ensures our predictions remain reliable and data-driven. Check our results page for more details on performance metrics.
Can I use your predictions for betting?
Our predictions are designed to provide data-driven insights for football outcomes. While they can help inform betting decisions, no prediction is guaranteed. Always bet responsibly and consider multiple factors before placing a wager.
What data does the model use for predictions?
The model analyzes historical match data, including team performance, goal statistics, shots, corners, and betting odds. By extracting key features, it identifies trends that influence match outcomes.
Why do some predictions have higher confidence than others?
Prediction confidence varies based on data consistency and match conditions. Stronger trends in historical data lead to higher confidence, while unpredictable matches—like evenly matched teams or key player absences—result in lower confidence scores.
